(1)
The three major types of joins in SQL are: CROSS JOIN, INNER JOIN, and OUTER JOIN. Additionally, other notable join types include: LEFT JOIN, RIGHT JOIN, and FULL JOIN. SQLite only supports LEFT (OUTER) JOIN, whereas execution of RIGHT JOIN and FULL JOIN requires a more complete version of SQL, such as SQL standard.
INNER JOIN is equivalent to the regular JOIN expression.
