Main Header for Section About Yosemite

Yosemite National Park is a park located in Northern California that is managed by the National Park Service. Millions of people visit the park year round, and the majority like to spend their time in Yosemite Valley. There are many things to do in Yosemite including seeing breath taking waterfalls, viewing gorgeous sights, and hiking.

Hours

Open 24 hours daily

Fees and Passes

entrance fees: $30

annual pass: $60

Directions

You can drive to Yosemite all year and enter via Highways 41, 140, and 120 from the west.
